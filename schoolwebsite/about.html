About Us

1. Our Lyceum's History

Jalkyz was founded in 1995 on the basis of an agreement between the Ministry of Education of the Kyrgyz Republic and the Sapat International Educational Center.

Our lyceum is fully accredited by Cambridge education, which proves the high standards of education in our lyceum. The students receive a high-quality education for 5 years and achieve not only academic success, but also develop their extracurricular activities.

3. Our Teachers

Jalkyz's teachers prepare individuals with stable human qualities in an ever-changing world, who benefit their country and the whole world with their education.

To do this, they take advanced training courses, participate in seminars, and also work on self- education and self-development

4. Our School Parliament

The School Parliament is one of the representative bodies of the student self-government system. The school parliament is formed from representatives of the collectives of the 10th grades on the basis of class elections.

They participate in determining the prospects for the development of the school, ways to increase the educational potential of the school, improve its life. They maintain school discipline and participate in the planning of the school.
