How to apply

From April 2023, entrance exams for 6th grade students at the high schools of the international educational institution "Sapat" will start in all regions of the republic.
The exam is held in two stages:

--- Stage 1 ---

The 1st round of the competition is held in the respective cities and districts, the questionnaire
prepared on the basis of the 6th grade program asks 50 questions, the questions are formed in
accordance with the Kyrgyz/Russian-speaking classes and cover the following subjects:


Mathematics - 20 questions
Kyrgyz language/Russian language - 10 questions
History - 4 questions
Geography - 4 questions
Biology - 4 questions
English - 8 questions

--- Stage 2 ---

Students who passed the 2nd round of the exam complete a task consisting of 25 questions in
mathematics and logic.


Mathematics - 15 questions
Logic - 10 questions
